groups Who We Are

Belagavi Organics is the marketing division of Belagavi District Regional Co‑Op Farmers Associations Federation Ltd., Belagavi — created to build social and economic sustainability for over 2500 small and marginal certified organic farmers in the Belagavi District.

By directly sourcing your food from these dedicated farmers, we ensure you receive the purest, hand-cleaned, chemical-free and pesticide-free organic products. Every grain is hygienically processed and truly healthy.
